# Citation & data license

The **code** in this repository is MIT-licensed (see `LICENSE`). It is built on the
**FlyWire** connectome of the adult *Drosophila melanogaster* (fruit fly) brain, whose
**data is licensed CC BY-NC 4.0 — non-commercial**. Anything you build with the data
(including this project when run with it) must therefore be **non-commercial** and must
attribute the sources below.

## Not affiliated
**flyputer is an independent hobby project.** It is **not affiliated with, nor endorsed by,**

## Please cite
If you use this project or the FlyWire data, cite:

- Dorkenwald, S., et al. (2024). *Neuronal wiring diagram of an adult brain.* **Nature 634.**
- Schlegel, P., et al. (2024). *Whole-brain annotation and multi-connectome cell typing of
  Drosophila.* **Nature.**
- Zheng, Z., Lauritzen, J. S., et al. (2018). *A complete electron microscopy volume of the
  brain of adult Drosophila melanogaster.* **Cell 174** (the underlying EM data).

The energy estimates reference: Attwell & Laughlin (2001), *An energy budget for signaling in
the grey matter of the brain*; Horowitz (2014), *Computing's energy problem*; and Landauer
(1961), *Irreversibility and heat generation in the computing process*.
